## Why not just use YouTube?

YouTube is built for discovery, not continuity.

- No progression between experiments  
- Foundational videos are easy to miss  
- No sense of completion  
- Constant distractions  

If you want random curiosity, it works.  
If you want to actually follow and understand the experiments, it doesn’t.
