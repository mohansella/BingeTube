## Why not just use YouTube?

YouTube is built for engagement, not structured learning.

- No clear progression  
- Older videos get buried  
- No tracking of what you’ve completed  
- Easy to get distracted  

If you're exploring casually, it works.  
If you want to truly understand concepts step by step, it doesn’t.
